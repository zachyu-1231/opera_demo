Five Types of Opera Performance

Cantonese Opera

This is a traditional Cantonese opera piece, known for its unique vocal style and intricate costumes.

Huangmei Opera

Huangmei opera is famous for its simple and beautiful melodies, originating from the Anhui province.

Peking Opera

Peking opera combines music, vocal performance, mime, dance, and acrobatics, and is the most celebrated of Chinese operas.

Shaanbei Opera

Shaanbei opera is known for its powerful and resonant singing style, originating from the Shaanxi province.

Yue Opera

Yue opera is popular in the Zhejiang province and is characterized by its lyrical and romantic style.
